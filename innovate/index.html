सहस्त्रकार (Sahastrakaar) - Breaking the shackles of disability



Our Mission

Empower individuals with special needs and remove barriers
to their active participation in society and make them self-reliant.

What is DrishtiPad?

DrishtiPad helps individuals with poor vision having difficulties avoiding hazards. It is a wearable gadget that would enable these people to go about their regular lives without relying on others.

What is unique about the DrishtiPad?

  • Our product, DrishtiPad, is compact, lightweight and highly efficient.
  • Being cost effective and user friendly, it is the ideal product for individuals with impaired vision.
  • DrishtiPad can detect pits and dangerous objects from afar. DrishtiPad has a wide range and can detect obstacles in a range of 3 metres from the individual, giving the person enough time to act accordingly.
  • DrishtiPad gives its users a sense of self reliance by being attached to the body via a belt. It is to be worn on the leg.
  • Our objective is to boost the self-esteem of persons with disabilities and lessen their daily struggles.
  • The primary goal is to develop a product that will aid them and allow the individuals in the age range of 15-45 years to multitask by using our product for navigation. Further, we wish to reach as many individuals as possible to receive criticism so we can continue to enhance our product.



Features Of DrishtiPad


Detect Deep pits

Detect Deep pits - The device can detect any lowering in the level of the ground whether it is an open manhole or a downward staircase or a cliff or a simple edge of the footpath.

Wide Range Coverage

Wide Range Coverage - It covers 128 deg.Our product is small and compact - With respect to other existing solutions, DrishtiPad is smaller thus it does not reduce the mobility of the user. It gets blended with the outfit in public places.

Walking Assistance

Walking assistance simply detects if any obstacle is in front of the user by a method of measurement distance and hindrance. Timely voice alerts are given to the user's precaution
